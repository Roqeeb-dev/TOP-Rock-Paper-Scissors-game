How to Play Rock, Paper, Scissors

Each player (you and the computer) chooses one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. You choose by clicking on either the rock, paper or scissors.

Winning rules

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper

Draw

If both players (You and the computer) choose the same option, it's a tie.

Scoring

  • ✅ 1 point is awarded for every win.
  • ⚖️ No points for a tie

How to win the game

The first to reach 3 points wins the round.
