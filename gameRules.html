Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to Play

  • 🎮 Choose your move: Rock 🪨, Paper 🧻, or Scissors ✂️
  • 🤖 The computer will randomly pick one too!
  • Press the r key to choose rock
  • Press the p key to choose paper
  • Press the s key to choose scissors
  • Press the n key to restart game
  • ✊ ~ Rock
  • ✋ ~ Paper
  • ✌️ ~ Scissors

Winning rules

  • ✊ Rock crushes ✌️ Scissors
  • ✋ Paper wraps ✊ Rock
  • ✌️ Scissors cuts ✋ Paper

Draw

If both players (You and the computer) choose the same option, it's a tie.

Scoring

  • ✅ 1 point is awarded for every win.
  • 🏆 Win to earn points, 😵 Lose and try again!
  • ⚖️ No points for a tie

How to win the game

🔄 First to 5 points wins the match!
